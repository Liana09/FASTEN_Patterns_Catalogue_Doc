A Catalogue of
Safety Case Patterns

As part of a Systematic Literature Review, a catalogue of 264
safety case patterns has been compiled, 34 of which have been
modelled and made available in FASTEN tool in order to facilitate
safety case development using patterns.

  • More About The Catalogue

Why are safety case patterns important?

To argue safety, information about the system, its environment and the development processes are
structured in the form of an argumentation that represents a safety case. As the development of safety cases
poses a significant challenge due to their complexity, the concept of safety case patterns has received
increasing interest from engineers, as means to reuse of successful argumentation structures.


  • View Catalogue

The patterns presented in the catalogue have been modelled and documented in FASTEN tool in order facilitate safety case development using patterns.

FASTEN is an open source environment for the specification, verification and assurance of critical systems. FASTEN is built with JetBrains' MPS and addresses different concerns:

  • • Requirements - textual specifications, natural language templates or mixing text and formal models;
  • • Design - different views over the architecture, high-level constructs for formal design based on NuSMV and Spin;
  • • Safety analyses - model-based safety analyses HARA, STPA, FMEA, HiP-HOPS, FT;
  • • Assurance arguments - use GSN, integrate with system and safety models, model confidence and deficits
